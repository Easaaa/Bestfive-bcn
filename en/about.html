About

Hi there!
If you're reading this, you're probably curious to know who we are and why we are making this project.

We are a couple living in Barcelona: Francisca works as a tour guide and Leonardo as a programmer.

We were interested to search for good places to eat and to enjoy our free time in Barcelona. Bad thing was that we found tons of review and most of them weren’t from local people. The problem with tourist's opinion is that often they don't know, for example, exactly how a specific meal needs to be cooked.

We decided to try to give for free our opinion to the tourists, a little guide from local citizens that love Barcelona and experiment foods and activities.

We hope that you find our application helpful and we are open to receive any kind of suggestions, thanks for your attention!
