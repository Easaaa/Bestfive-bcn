Hi there! If you're reading this, you're probably curious to know who we are and why we are making this project.

We were streesed to search good place to eat and to enjoy our free time, we were bored to let choose to other people from internet, nowdays a lot of review are not from local people, but from tourist that often they don't know, for example, how exactly a specific meal needs to be cooked.

For that we decided to try to give for free our opinion to the tourist, a little guide from local citizens that love Barcelona and experiment foods and activites.

We hope that you find our application helpfull, we are open to recive any kind of suggestions, thanks for your attention!
