La cova fumada

  • € 12 Average price.

This is the most local place you can find in Barceloneta. Not the cleanest, nothing fancy and It is very very noisy! There are 8 tables and a bar where you can eat, its always full of people. They serve the most authentic food. Try seafood here and the artichoke! Wow! Cheap.

Can paixano

  • € 9,80 Average price.

Traditional cava and “embutidos” catalan bar. Very small place, full of people standing, you have to yell to the barman what you want. They only serve cava (catalan champgne) and embutidos (sausages, chorizo, cheese). Eveything is local and definitely worth the try. They close at 22:00. Cheap.

Bar celta pulperia

  • € 14,50 Average price.

Go and eat octopus, our favorite place! This is a bar from Galicia (north-west spain) they have the typical Galician tapas. All the seafood here is fantastic! Try “navajas” and of course “pulpo a la gallega” you can also drink the Galician wine that they serve you in typical Galician cups, each cup only cost 1,5€.

La bombeta

  • € 15,20 Average price.

The bar that has the best “bomba” in Bcn. Bomba is a potato ball filled with meat and has a spicy sauce on top. They also make very good pimientos del padrón (green peppers). They have no wifi, they don’t speak English and its a bit messy but worth the try!
