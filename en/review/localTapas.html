La Cova Fumada

  • Mon - Fri 9:00 AM - 3:15 PM
  • Thu - Fri 6:00 PM - 8:15 PM
  • Sat 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM
  • Sunday closed

GO!

A lady cooks here, like it was her home. The most amazing local place! Its very noisy, you may have to sit on the bar because it's always full.

Can Paixano

  • Mon - Sat 9:00 AM - 10:30 PM
  • Sunday closed

GO!

Small yet crowded bar. You will have to eat-drink standing. They have their own "cava" (Catalan Champagne) and the most typical catalan sausages, ham and cheese. Very cheap!

Celta Pulperia

  • Monday closed
  • Tue - Sun 12:00 PM - 12:00 AM

GO!

Octopus and seafood here are fabulous. Typical Galician bar. Glass of white wine is 1,5€ and you have to try "Pulpo a la Gallega" and "Navajas". Classic, local, great!

Bodega Vasconia

  • Monday closed
  • Tue - Fri 2:00 PM - 12:00 AM
  • Sat - Sun 1:00 PM - 12:00 AM

GO!

Old bar from 1905, Small, modest, cheap and the food is amazing. we recommend to try "Rabo de toro" and "buñuelos de bacalao con miel". Also the staff is super nice and funny.

La Bombeta

  • Thu - Tue 10:00 AM - 12:00 AM
  • Wednesday closed

GO!

No wifi, waiters have a hard time speaking english but this is one of the most traditional places in Barcelona: one of the first bars to create "la bomba" (catalan tapas) made with potato and meat.
