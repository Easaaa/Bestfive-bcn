Bar Central

  • Mon - Sun 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM

GO!

This place is amazing! It’s got a beautiful fountain, the menu has fantastic sandwiches and it’s not expensive. Definitely one of the top hidden gems of Barcelona.

El Jardí

  • Mon - Sun 10:00 AM - 12:00 AM

GO!

Hidden terrace inside an ex-hospital building of the 1400s (where Gaudí died). The ambiance is great, waiters are sexy and music is good. We recommend going for drinks.

Antic Teatre

  • Mon - Thu 10:00 AM - 11:30 PM
  • Fri 10:00 AM - 12:00 AM
  • Sat 5:00 PM - 12:00 AM
  • Sun 5:00 PM - 11:30 PM

GO!

Not Fancy, you have to go to the bar to get your drinks but this place has real magic. Its very hidden, known by locals and suuuper cheap!

Flax and Kale

  • Mon - Fri 9:00 AM - 11:30 PM
  • Sat - Sun 9:30 AM - 11:30 PM

GO!

This terrace is chic, modern, and beautiful. The food is good, lots of veggie options. It’s a chill out bar terrace to escape the city centre traffic.

La Caseta
del Migdia

  • Closing hours may change
  • Mon- Tue closed
  • Wed 9:00 PM - 12:00 AM
  • Thu 8:00 PM - 1:00 AM
  • Fri 8:00 PM - 1:00 AM
  • Sat 12:00 PM - 1:00 AM

GO!

Definitely worth to go if you are going up Montjuic Mountain. Very rustic, this bar is inside the forest, just next to it the most amazing views of all the industrial port of Barcelona. Its not at all known, nothing fancy but it’s got a great vibe. On weekends they do barbecue lunch.
