del Mar

GO!
8€

Very beautiful church built by the townspeople in only 54 years, (1329-1383), most important characters where "los bastaixos", Catalan workers who carried all the stones on their backs from the quarry in montjuic to the location. After the Civil War, FC Barcelona (football team) donated a lot of money to its reconstruction and one of its stained glass windows has the team's shield. Can you find it?
