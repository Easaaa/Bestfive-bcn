Federal Café

  • Weekly Hours
  • Mon - Sat 9:00 AM - 08:00 PM
  • Sun 09:00 AM - 05:30 PM

GO!

This is a very trendy and very hipster place. They have two venues and the coffee is really good. Their highlight is breakfast: eggs, avocado, salmon, pancakes, carrot cake, brownies; and everything is amazing. It is also a great place to work with your computer since they have a big table with plugs and all.
