If you are looking for high-quality local design, Born is the perfect place. El Raval

  • Weekly Hours
  • Monday closed
  • Tue - Sun 12:00 PM - 12:00 AM

GO!
Shop

This is Barcelona’s underground spot. It is perfect if you are looking to find vintage and second-hand clothing stores. Some of the famous stores are: Flamingo, Hola la, Lullaby, PNB, Love Vintage. There is a street called “Career Rivera Baixa” with many shops that sell clothes per kilo.

Paseo De Gracia

  • Weekly Hours
  • Monday closed
  • Tue - Fri 2:00 PM - 12:00 AM
  • Sat - Sun 1:00 PM - 12:00 AM

GO!
Shop

If you are looking for top-notch luxury, this is your street: Channel, Cloe, Hermes, Dior, Fendi, you name it. The street is wide, open and stunning. If you decide to visit, don’t forget to pass by Antoni Gaudí’s most famous houses: Casa Milá and Casa Battle.

This is Barcelona’s most transited pedestrian street. It is a wide esplanade with great number of small and large stores like Zara, Mango, Oysho, Bershka, etc. People enjoy shopping there as everything is at plain sight and easy to access.
