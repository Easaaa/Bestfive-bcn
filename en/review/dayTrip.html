Cadaques
•Casa Dali

It takes about 2,5h to get there by car or 3,5h by public transport but this is totally worth it. Cadaques is a dream Spanish white town in the middle of a beautiful national protected park. The house where Salvador Dalí lived is there and it is amazing!

Girona
•Museo Dali

Girona is a medieval City, it has one of the most beautiful cathedrals in all Spain. Part of Game of Thrones series was filmed there. Half an hour from Girona you can go to Figueras where the Dalí museum is located. This is a surreal, dynamic, museum where Dalí donated most of his work.

San Pol de Mar

Little village an hour from Barcelona, We recommend to get there by train and then walk towards Calella beach skirting the coast until you get to Cala Roca Grossa. This place is a little beach paradise.

Montserrat

This is a monastery dedicated to a Black Madona in the middle of a very strange rock formation mountain. You can visit the monastery and walk some trekking routes there. Beautiful day trip!

Sitges

A beach village only 40 minutes from Bcn by train. Beautiful beaches, lots of shops and bars to hang out. One of the easiest places to go and always have fun.
