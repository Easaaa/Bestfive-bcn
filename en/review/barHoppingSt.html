Carrer de Blai

GO!
x€

In this street you will find many bars that serve “Pinxos”, which are a piece of bread with many ingredients on them. You can go barhop and try many. They are all about 1, 5 euro each. Next to Blai street there is “Quimet I Quimet” Tapas bar, definitely worth visiting.
